Frequently Asked Questions

How do subscription plans work?

Arova plans cover both partners in a shared space. Only one partner needs to upgrade to Pro or Platinum for the upgrade to apply to both profiles.

Is my data secure in the Sandbox?

Yes, your data is stored securely in your local browser sandbox context. The simulated checkout processes pricing configurations locally, and no real billing info is stored or processed.

Can I switch plans later?

Absolutely. You can change your active tier in local mode settings at any time or upgrade your sandbox demo package using the checkout links.
